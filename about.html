About Me

Welcome, My name is Justin Picard, and I currently live near Mount Vernon with my wife, daughter, and son. I like to put together and paint plastic tabletop miniatures and models. I have done a few small projects in my woodshop; I am still an amateur in woodworking, but I am eager to learn. I have worked as a help desk personnel, for about 15 years and the prior 4 years as a metal worker for the US Army. I was born and raised in Louisiana, joined the military in 2006, I am now SFC mentoring other NCO's and have an approved retirement date next year. I plan to earn an Associate Degree of Science in Computer Technology before I retire and then I plan to continue and earn a Bachelor degree of Science in Computer Technology focusing on Web Design. The content of this course is relevant to my interests in Computer Technology academics.

With retirement from military just around the corner, I have been deep diving into looking at houses, colleges, and career opportunities for both me and my wife. I am aware it is too early to view each of those as when it comes time, the options will most likely be way different. We still have uncertainties, but I feel that browsing the options may assist me in planning, especially knowing that there are decisions that must be made to get through the retirement process. It will work out, and when the time comes, we will adjust as necessary.
