Application Prototype Informational Page


Never miss a dose. Take control of your health

Welcome to All-in-One Rx Management, your all-in-one digital companion for managing prescriptions, tracking refills, and receiving medication reminders.


Key Features

Smart Medication Reminders
Set custom alarms for your medication dosage, frequency, and timing.

One-Tap Refill Requests
Request repeat prescriptions directly from your phone and send them securely to your pharmacy.

Real-Time Prescription Tracking
Know exactly where your medication is with the pharmacy.

Scan-to-Add Medication
Instantly add new prescriptions by scanning the barcode or taking a photo of your medication label.

Family and Caregiver Sync
Manage prescriptions for children or elderly family members from a single, secure account.

Secure and HIPAA Compliant
Your health data is encrypted and protected.

How It Works

  1. Register: Securely log in and link your local pharmacy and provider.
  2. Add Medications: Add your current prescriptions manually or by scanning.
  3. Receive Reminders: Get alerts when it's time to take your medicine.
  4. Order and Track: Order refills and watch the status in real-time.

Why Use All-in-One Rx Management

  • Saves Time: Avoid unnecessary trips to the pharmacy.
  • Improves Consistency: Reduces missed doses and refills.
  • Clear Visibility: Know your prescription status from provider to pharmacy.

Prototype Context and Research Basis

  • Purpose: To improve medication consistency and reduce the burden of the provider services by giving patients visibility of their prescriptions.
  • User Interface focus: Clean, modern, and intuitive mobile experience with a focus on simplicity.
  • Key Data Points: Includes demographic data, history, clinical data, and side effect management.
  • Prototype Features: Dependable logs, reminder alerts, and communication with pharmacies.
