User Guide


Managing Your Prescriptions

Welcome to the All-in-One Rx Management Prototype. This guide will help you view, request, and track your repeat prescriptions securely.

  1. Viewing Your Current Prescriptions
  2. Requesting a Repeat Prescription
  3. Selecting a Pharmacy
  4. Prescription Status Tracker
  5. Troubleshooting and Support

Viewing Your Current Prescriptions

Upon logging in, you will be taken to your Dashboard.

  • Active Medications: A list of all your current, active prescriptions.
  • Next Due Date: Shows when reorder is available.
  • Medication Details: Click on a medication name to view dosage instructions, remaining refills, and last date refilled.

Requesting a Repeat Prescription

  1. Select Items: Go to the Request Prescription" tab.
  2. Tick Items: Tick the box nexty to the medication(s) you wish to order.
  3. Special Request: If you need an item not on your list, use the "Special Request" box to type the item name.
  4. Confirm Request: Click the "Send Request" button.
  5. Confirmation: You will receive an on-screen confirmation and an email confirming the request has been sent to your doctor.

Selecting a Pharmacy

  • Change Pharmacy: You can nominate a pharmacy to receive your electronic prescription (EPS) directly. Go to Settings > Pharmacy to choose a new location.
  • Update Details: Ensure your phone number and email are current to receive notifcations about your order status.

Prescription Status Tracker

Track your request from approval to collection:

  • Requested: Submitted to your doctor.
  • Approved: Approved by the doctor and sent to the pharmacy.
  • Ready for Collection: The pharmacy has prepared your medication

Troubleshooting and Support

  • "Items not listed": If a medication is not showing, it may be due for a review. Please contact your Provider
  • Incorrect dosage: If a dosage is wrong, do not order it. Contact your Provider.
  • Forgotten Password: Click "Forgot Password" on the lgin screen to reset your credentials.
